Licensing Information

FusionCharts is licensed on a per-developer and per-website basis. There are five different types of licenses for businesses and developers.

For Businesses

  • Enterprise License
    For businesses having multiple websites and developers, or who want to deploy FusionCharts in their Intranet or wish to get complete source code for modification & re-compilation
  • Site License
    For businesses having single website with multiple developers or website that is hosted on SaaS /ASP model (charging a subscription fees from their customers)

For Individual Developers

  • Professional License
    For freelancers or professionals who do bespoke (custom) development for multiple clients
  • Developer License
    For individual developers who wish to implement FusionCharts on their websites, which are not hosted with a subscription model (maximum 3 websites)
  • User License
    For designers or general users who want to plot basic charts for static web pages and PowerPoint Presentations in a single website

Each license of FusionCharts allows unlimited development servers, unlimited test servers, unlimited CPUs on a given server and unlimited end viewers.
